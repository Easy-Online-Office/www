Frequently asked questions

Short answers to the questions customers ask most often.

Is Easy Office Online cloud-based?

Yes. Your entire workspace lives in the cloud and is accessible via any modern web browser.

Can we use our own domain name?

Absolutely. We can configure Easy Office Online to run behind your own branded domain, like office.yourcompany.com.

How long does onboarding take?

Most small teams are fully up and running within a week. Larger organisations may take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity.

How do you handle data security?

We follow industry best practices: encrypted storage and transit, role-based permissions, and detailed audit logs of critical actions.

Can we cancel anytime?

Yes. Our monthly plans are contract-free. Enterprise customers can negotiate terms that match their governance policies.

Do you offer training for our staff?

Yes. We can provide live remote training sessions and on-demand tutorials tailored to your workflows.
